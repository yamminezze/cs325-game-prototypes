Pong: Escalation 2.0

  • Originality: A variation of pong except over time the ball gets faster and faster
  • Technical Merit: The game becomes more and more intense as the game goes on.
  • Prototype Postmortem: I was limited by my lack of knowledge of the physics in the game engine. But I know much more now that ive created this game.
  • Prototype Assets: Assets came from google images and GMU library databases: https://library.prosoundeffects.com/#!explorer?s=bounce
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: This is a version of pong where the ball increases speed, increasing difficulty.
  • High Concept: Outplay your opponent until they make a mistake
  • Theme: ?
  • Mandated Variety: The input is the up and down arrow keys as well as "W" and "S". This tests reaction time
  • Prototype Goal: Ball bouncing physics and user input measurment. Also sprite interaction.
  • Player Experience Goals: Having the satisfaction of out-scoring your opponent.
  • Gameplay: Click to start and launch the ball. Player 1 controls with "W" and "S" player 2 controls with the up and down arrow keys. Basic pong rules but ball gets faster over time.
  • Strategies: Knowing where to bouce the ball so that the opponent doesnt have time to react.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: This game doesn't have much story. Thats up to your imagination.
  • Target Audience: This game is great for all ages and levels of experience. Mostly for competitive players
  • Play Time: A couple of minutes Version by Yammin Ezzedine
